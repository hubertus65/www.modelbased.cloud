What to Expect

Once you’ve filled out the Request a Demo form, our team will reach out within 1-2 business days to set up an information-gathering session. During this 15 – 30-minute session, our team will ask you about your system design challenges and use cases to ensure we understand how to serve you best. 

After an initial information gathering session, if Modelon’s solutions are a fit for your business needs, our team will schedule a personalized demo for you and your team.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does Modelon offer a free trial of Modelon Impact?

    Yes, Modelon offers a free evaluation period of Modelon Impact to qualified prospects so you and your colleagues can experience the tool hands-on.

    During an evaluation, you’ll work together with Modelon to determine key objectives that would make your evaluation a success. These objectives can include topics such as: 

    • Gaining experience working with a sample model
    • Understanding parameterization requirements
    • Diving into details of specific components
    • Performing a meaningful engineering analysis

    To see if you qualify for an evaluation, please request a demo and a sales representative will be in contact with you shortly. 

  • What experience or education do I need to use Modelon Impact?

    In our experience, customers that have a strong understanding of system modeling lead s to a fast path to success with Modelon Impact. In addition, the required experience or education depends on your specific use case and application.

    But don’t worry, we’re here to help! 

    Modelon offers a variety of services and training courses to help users get the most out of Modelon Impact. Our customer support teams are located globally, making support requests a quick and reliable process. Modelon also provides comprehensive documentation and guides within the  Modelon Help Center to guide users through the modeling and simulation process in Modelon Impact .

  • Does Modelon Impact integrate with other tools in my toolchain?

    Yes, easily integrate Modelon Impact into your existing in-house and commercial tools via REST APIs . Directly access the Modelica code behind your models , r un notebooks , and script simulations directly from the pre-installed Jupy terLab env ironment. In addition, import and export models as FMUs – a model file exchange type built upon the Functional Mockup Interface standardized format .

  • How does Modelon protect client data?

    Modelon Impact ensures exclusive access to user content. If content is shared between users, it is done through their own external code versioning system and access permissions are granted accordingly. C ommunication between Modelon Impact ’s cloud server and users ’web browser is always encrypted, but Modelon recommends exercising caution when sharing important company or personal data. To ensure security, Modelon Impact uses HTTPS for all services, regularly audits its implementation, and never stores customer passwords.
